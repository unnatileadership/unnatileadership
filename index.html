About Unnati

Coaching that meets you where you are.

Unnati — meaning progress and rise in Sanskrit — was founded on a simple belief: that every person has untapped potential waiting to be unlocked. Real growth isn't about adding more; it's about clearing what's in the way.

Whether you're a seasoned executive navigating a pivotal career moment or a teenager figuring out who you want to be, Unnati Leadership creates a space that is warm, honest, and genuinely personalized.

Our coaching draws from evidence-based frameworks, positive psychology, and deep human connection — because lasting change always starts from within.

Early Career Coaching Program

For professionals in their first 1–3 years at work — structured, practical, and deeply human.

The skills that matter most aren't taught in school.

Starting your career is exciting — and overwhelming. Most early professionals are figuring out workplace dynamics, communication, time management, and career direction entirely on their own.

The Unnati Early Career Program is a 6-week, 1:1 coaching journey that gives new professionals a structured framework to build confidence, capability, and clarity at exactly the right moment.

Each week focuses on a distinct theme, with guided coaching sessions, practical activities, and a downloadable deliverable that becomes part of your personal professional toolkit.

What you'll walk away with

Clarity on your values, strengths, and professional identity
A personal communication style guide
A stakeholder map and workplace navigation strategy
Your own productivity system — built around how you actually work
A networking tracker and authentic relationship strategy
A written 12–18 month career roadmap with accountability plan
Week 1 Foundation & Self-Awareness
Values clarification · Assessment · Your professional 'why'
Week 2 Communication & Presence
Executive presence · Email mastery · Asking for what you need
Week 3 Navigating the Workplace
Stakeholder mapping · Managing up · Unwritten rules
Week 4 Performance & Productivity
Prioritization frameworks · Deep work habits · Boundaries
Week 5 Networking & Visibility
Authentic relationships · LinkedIn presence · Informational interviews
Week 6 Vision & Momentum
Career roadmap · Personal board of advisors · 90-day plan
